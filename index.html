About Me

My name is Lathisha Nair and I was born in Kerela, South India but grew up in New Delhi. Around the age of 8 years old, my family moved to Arizona due to my mom's job and I have been here ever since.

I originally wanted to become a Pharmacist so I got my first job as a Pharmacy Technician and quickly found out that even though it is my passion to learn about the medical field, it isn't a career that I can see myself in anymore.

While working for PillPack, the pharmacy delivery company owned by Amazon, I was typing scripts on a computer all day and it made me curious to explore their system and that's what eventually led me to start a new career path. I loved being able to figure out tricks to most effieciently work and saw how troubleshooting issues was exciting. I also had a great time helping others with all the tips and tricks I found!
